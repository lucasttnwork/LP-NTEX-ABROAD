## **SECTION 3: HOW ELEE WORKS IN PRACTICE**

### Section Headline:
## The 4 Phases That Turn Ad Spend Into Predictable Revenue

### **Phase 1: EXPLORATION (Days 1-10)**
**"Test Wide, Fail Fast, Learn Faster"**

Traditional agencies test 2-3 ads and call it done. We test 10-20 variations simultaneously:

**What we test:**
- **4-6 offer angles** (¬£50 off vs Free consultation vs Limited slots vs Exclusive package)
- **3-4 audience segments** (Lookalikes, interests, demographics, behavioural)
- **3-4 creative formats** (Static, video, carousel, UGC-style)
- **2-3 copy frameworks** (Problem-focused, benefit-focused, social proof-focused)

**Powered by AI:** Our AI systems generate and deploy test variations 5x faster than manual creation, meaning we learn in days what others learn in weeks.

**Output:** We identify 2-3 "diamonds in the rough" ‚Äî combinations that show promise but need refinement.

---

### **Phase 2: LAPIDATION (Days 10-15)**
**"Polish the Diamonds"**

Now we take those 2-3 winning combinations and perfect them through micro-optimisation:

**Surgical A/B Testing:**
- Test headline variations (keeping everything else identical)
- Test image adjustments (same message, different visual)
- Test CTA buttons (Book Now vs Get Started vs Learn More)
- Test urgency levels (soft vs hard deadlines)

**The Lapidation Rule:** Change ONE variable at a time. Measure precisely. Keep what works.

**AI Advantage:** Our AI analyses performance patterns across 50+ data points to identify which micro-changes drive macro results.

**Output:** We reduce cost per booking by 20-40% through systematic refinement.

---

### **Phase 3: ESCALATION (Days 15-25)**
**"Bet Big on Certainty"**

This is where 70% of your budget goes ‚Äî into the proven winners.

**How we scale without breaking what works:**
- Gradual budget increases (20% daily max)
- Audience expansion using Meta's algorithm
- Geographic testing (expanding from city to region)
- Placement optimisation (feed, stories, reels)

**The key:** We only scale AFTER we have statistical proof, not hopes.

**Output:** Volume increases 3-5x while maintaining or improving cost per booking.

### **Phase 4: EXTRACTION (Ongoing)**
